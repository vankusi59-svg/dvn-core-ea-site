FAQ

Before you
automate.

Why does checkout happen on MQL5?+

MQL5 handles the purchase, license activations and product updates. This site helps you compare the systems; the official Market remains the source of truth for availability, price and license terms.

Can I test an EA before buying?+

Yes. Use the free demo in MetaTrader 5 Strategy Tester. Test with realistic spread, commission and broker conditions before making a decision.

Which EA should I start with?+

Choose by trading logic, not by price. XAU Sniper Pro suits selective gold reversals; Trendline Breakout suits structural trend moves; Squid X suits traders who explicitly accept a bounded two-position recovery model.

Do these systems remove trading risk?+

No. Every EA can lose. Broker execution, spread, slippage and changing market regimes can make live results differ from tests. Start on demo and size risk conservatively.

Do I need a VPS?+

A VPS is strongly recommended for stable 24/5 operation. It reduces missed ticks and interruptions, but it cannot guarantee execution quality or results.
