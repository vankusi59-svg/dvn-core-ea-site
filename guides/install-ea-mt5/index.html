How to install an Expert Advisor on MT5

A practical installation checklist for adding an MQL5 Market EA to MetaTrader 5, enabling automated trading and verifying the first launch safely.

Use this guide to verify process—not to predict performance.Updated 18 July 2026
STEP BY STEP
  1. 01

    Use the correct MT5 account

    Open MetaTrader 5 and confirm the broker server, account type and trading account are the ones you intend to test.

  2. 02

    Sign in to MQL5 inside MT5

    Connect the same MQL5 account that owns the product license. Do not share account passwords or activation details.

  3. 03

    Install from the Market section

    Open the Market or Navigator purchase list, locate the EA and complete the platform installation process.

  4. 04

    Attach the EA to the intended chart

    Open the required symbol and timeframe, then attach the EA. Follow the product page rather than guessing the chart requirements.

  5. 05

    Review every input

    Check lot sizing, risk mode, trading hours, spread limits and symbol-specific settings before allowing orders.

  6. 06

    Enable algorithmic trading

    Enable Algo Trading only after the settings are reviewed. Confirm the EA status and read the Experts and Journal tabs for errors.

  7. 07

    Run demo before live

    Keep the first deployment on a demo account. Verify order size, stop-loss behavior, symbol suffixes and broker execution before considering live use.

FINAL CHECKLIST

Before you continue.

  • Correct broker server, symbol and timeframe
  • MQL5 license installed under the owning account
  • Risk and lot-size settings reviewed
  • Algo Trading permission enabled only after review
  • Experts and Journal tabs free from blocking errors
  • Demo validation completed before live deployment

Historical tests and demo operation do not guarantee live results.
