HOW IT FITS

Built for a specific operating style.

Squid X is an XAUUSD H1 Expert Advisor that combines momentum logic with one optional equal-size recovery position. It never multiplies lot size and caps exposure at two positions.

Best fit: Gold traders who understand recovery mechanics and explicitly accept the risk of holding two correlated positions.

LOGIC SUMMARY

What the system is designed to do.

  1. Uses H1 gold momentum as the primary directional framework.
  2. Allows only one additional recovery position when its rules are met.
  3. Keeps the second position equal in size rather than multiplying lots.
KNOWN LIMITATIONS

Understand the downside before deployment.
